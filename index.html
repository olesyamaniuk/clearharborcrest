About the Experience

Relaxing Fireplace - Meditate is not about interaction or progression. It’s about creating a calm visual space where users can pause and reset.
We kept it super simple on purpose – you literally just open it and stare at the fire. No buttons to press, no levels to unlock - just flames doing their thing.
